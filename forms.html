Client Resources

Forms & Documents

Access the standard forms used for MyBark's services. All forms must be submitted by registered clients only.

Available Forms

Service Forms

Client Registration Form

This form must be completed and verified before any MyBark services can be accessed. It collects your identity, contact and purpose of engagement details.

  • Personal identification details
  • Contact information
  • Nature of services required
  • KYC documentation attachment
Request Form

Power of Attorney Mandate

Required when authorising MyBark to act as your power of attorney. This document specifies the scope and limitations of the authorisation.

  • Clearly defined scope of authority
  • Duration and termination conditions
  • Notarisation requirements
  • Witness signature fields
Request Form

Document Deposit Form

Use this form when lodging documents for secure safekeeping. Each deposited document is logged and assigned a unique reference number.

  • Document description and type
  • Access authorisation list
  • Retrieval conditions
  • Storage duration preference
Request Form

Property Management Form

Required to initiate property record keeping services. Covers rental properties, land holdings and commercial premises across India.

  • Property address and type
  • Ownership documentation
  • Tenancy details (if applicable)
  • Compliance and filing requirements
Request Form

Fee Payment Mandate

Authorise MyBark to make fee payments to specified agencies on your behalf. Each mandate is limited to the agencies and amounts you specify.

  • Agency or institution name
  • Payment type and frequency
  • Approved payment limits
  • Reporting and confirmation preferences
Request Form
